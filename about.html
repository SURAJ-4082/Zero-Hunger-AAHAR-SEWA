Welcome to AAHAR SEWA!

We are Team Jawan, a group of nine passionate BCS students committed to making a difference through technology and community service. Assigned with the task of creating a web app aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), we chose to focus on SDG 2: Zero Hunger. This led to the birth of AAHAR SEWA, a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and alleviating hunger in our community.

Our Mission

At AAHAR SEWA, our mission is to bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need. We aim to create a sustainable solution to hunger and food waste by connecting donors and recipients through our user-friendly web app. Our initiatives are designed to raise awareness about food security, promote healthy eating habits, and encourage community involvement.

Our Initiatives

1. No Sugar Challenge: Encouraging youths to cut sugar from their diets to promote physical well-being, mental clarity, and emotional stability.
2. Check the Label: Educating people about the importance of reading food labels to make informed nutritional choices and avoid unhealthy food options.

Our Journey

Our journey began with a shared vision and a commitment to creating positive change. Through collaborative efforts, we have developed AAHAR SEWA to serve as a catalyst for reducing food waste and providing nutritious meals to the underprivileged. Each member of Team Jawan has brought unique skills and perspectives, making our project a true team effort. Together, we strive to create a meaningful impact and contribute to a world where no one goes hungry. We believe that through collective action and innovative solutions, we can achieve a sustainable future for all. Thank you for supporting AAHAR SEWA. Join us in our mission to end hunger and make a difference in our community.
